The Best Movies According to Chung

My top 3 movies of all-time.

God Father Movie Series

The Godfather is a trilogy of American crime films directed by Francis Ford Coppola inspired by the 1969 novel of the same name by Italian American author Mario Puzo.

Close Encounters of the Third Kind

It is the story of Roy Neary, an everyday blue-collar worker in Indiana, whose life changes after an encounter with a UFO.

Jurassic Park

Jurassic Park is a 1993 American science fiction action film directed by Steven Spielberg.
